The Material

Tracing
The Source.

Fishing nets are made from highly durable nylon, designed to withstand the harshest ocean conditions. When discarded, they become deadly "ghost nets" that trap marine life for centuries.

We built a fully traceable and equitable supply chain mapping directly to the source. Creating NetPlus®—a 100% recycled nylon material that performs perfectly while drastically reducing global carbon emissions.
